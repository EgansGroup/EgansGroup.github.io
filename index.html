Our People

Andy Egan

Director • Egans Group Ltd

A customer‑focused, profit‑driven leader with over 25 years of experience in the aerospace and aviation industry, Andy founded Egans Group Ltd to bring world‑class technical leadership and innovation to aviation organisations globally.

Before establishing Egans Group, Andy spent 25 years at easyJet, one of Europe’s largest and most innovative airlines, holding senior technology and leadership roles across operations, systems, and digital transformation. His career has been defined by delivering complex, high‑impact projects on time, within budget, and with measurable operational and commercial value.
